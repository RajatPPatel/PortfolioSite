About

I am a senior, results-driven Lead Game Developer specializing in Unity (C#), multiplayer architecture (Photon Fusion, Mirror, AWS GameLift), and real-time physics-driven gameplay. I focus on designing scalable game systems, custom physics models, and low-latency netcode for high-concurrency multiplayer games.

Over the last 7+ years, I’ve shipped multiple titles across Android, iOS, PC, WebGL, and VR, leading development from rapid prototyping to global launch. I excel at solving complex engineering challenges, such as integrating custom hardware motion inputs (IoT sensors) and deploying dedicated servers globally with AWS GameLift.

To deliver premium products rapidly, I actively integrate advanced AI assistant workflows—including Claude Code, Cursor, GPT, and MCP (Model Context Protocol)—along with custom Editor tools to automate pipelines, write clean, maintainable architecture, and accelerate iteration cycles.
